Compass

Ritchie Navigation Instruments (MA, 781-826-5131), Model D515, serial number C80. Rebuilt 2006.

To remove the compass, take the two screws out that hold on the light bracket. Then, lift the housing for the compass straight up and away from the pedestal. Mark the orientation of the compass before loosening the screw clamp.
