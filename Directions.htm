Directions to Willow

Willow is located in Pasadena, Maryland on Rock Creek. Rock Creek is on the south shore of the Patapsco River half-way between the Key Bridge and the Chesapeake Bay. Willow is located at:

Oak Harbor Marina
1343 Old Water Oak Point Road
Pasadena, MD 21122

To get to Willow, follow the appropriate directions:

Coming From the South on Rt. 95:
  1. Take Rt. 95 north to Rt. 100 East (Gibson Island, Glen Burnie).
  2. Go 15.4 miles and make a left turn on Magothy Bridge Road (Rt. 607N).
  3. Continue with the directions below from Magothy Bridge Road.
Coming From the South on Rt. 97:
  1. Take Rt. 97 north to Rt. 100 East (Gibson Island, Glen Burnie)
  2. Go 7.6 miles and make a left turn on Magothy Bridge Road (Rt. 607N).
  3. Continue with the directions below from Magothy Bridge Road.
Coming From the Baltimore Beltway (Rt. 695):
  1. Take Rt.10 south to Rt. 100 East (Gibson Island, Glen Burnie)
  2. Go 3.2 miles and make a left turn on Magothy Bridge Road (Rt. 607N).
  3. Continue with the directions below from Magothy Bridge Road.
Directions From Magothy Bridge Road:
  1. Go 1.1 miles on Magothy Bridge Road and make a right turn onto Ft. Smallwood Road.
  2. Go 0.5 miles and turn left on Water Oak Point Road.
  3. Go 0.5 miles and turn left on Old Water Oak Point Road.
  4. Go 0.4 miles and turn left into the marina driveway (1343 -- watch the speed humps!).
  5. Go down the driveway and into the marina.
  6. Bear right and follow the driveway towards the water.
  7. Park your car wherever you can near the marina office. Be sure not to block the TravelLift.
Willow is on B dock at slip 84:
  1. From the picnic area at the marina office, walk down the concrete and wood ramp onto the main dock.
  2. Walk out to the end of the dock.
  3. At the end, turn left and walk towards the end of the dock.
  4. Willow is in the third slip on the right side of the dock.

See you there!
