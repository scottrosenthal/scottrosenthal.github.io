Willow Hurricane Preparation

  • All sails down and stored below
  • Dodger and awning down and stored below
  • Wheel cover on
  • Dorade cowl vents off and caps installed
  • Secure cabin and V-berth hatches on all sides
  • Unplug AC power
  • Turn off AC power at meter
  • Move all deck cushions below
  • Double docklines
  • Add chafing gear to all docklines
  • Add spring lines to starboard side
  • Use springs to position Willow in the center of the slip
  • Put fenders out around Willow
  • Take dinghy ashore to use to get out to Willow
